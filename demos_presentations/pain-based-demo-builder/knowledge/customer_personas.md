# Customer Personas (placeholder)

## IT Director
**Cares about:** Uptime, reduced ticket volume, team efficiency.
**Demo emphasis:** Reliability, centralized management, automation.

## CISO / Security Lead
**Cares about:** Risk reduction, detection speed, audit readiness.
**Demo emphasis:** Threat detection, compliance reporting, incident response.

## CFO / Finance Stakeholder
**Cares about:** Predictable cost, ROI, risk of downtime/breach translated to dollars.
**Demo emphasis:** executive_demo.md template — business impact table, ROI metrics.

## Network/Systems Engineer
**Cares about:** Technical depth, integration details, edge cases.
**Demo emphasis:** technical_demo.md template — architecture, live troubleshooting.
