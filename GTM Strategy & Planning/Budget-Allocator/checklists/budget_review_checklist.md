# Budget Review Checklist

**Purpose of this file**: A checklist for reviewing a completed GTM budget allocation model before it is finalized or presented to stakeholders.

---

## Completeness

- [ ] Total budget, planning horizon, and currency are stated clearly
- [ ] Every channel under consideration is listed, including any not currently funded
- [ ] Prior-period spend, CAC, and ROI/ROAS are shown for each channel (or explicitly marked as unavailable)
- [ ] Proposed spend is shown for each channel in both dollar amount and percentage of total
- [ ] A reserve/contingency line is included or its absence is explicitly justified
- [ ] Fixed/non-channel costs are accounted for separately from channel spend

## Reconciliation

- [ ] Sum of proposed channel spend plus reserve equals the total stated budget
- [ ] Bottom-up channel totals are reconciled against any top-down ceiling
- [ ] Any gap between stated growth targets and what the budget can realistically achieve is explicitly disclosed

## Rationale

- [ ] Every increase or decrease in channel spend is tied to a specific metric (CAC, ROI, payback period, or marginal return signal)
- [ ] Marginal (not just average) return is considered for any channel proposed for a significant spend increase
- [ ] Payback tier (fast/medium/slow) is assigned to each channel
- [ ] Channel mix reflects the buyer profile (deal size, account concentration, sales cycle) rather than defaulting to last year's split without review

## Risk

- [ ] Concentration risk is flagged if any single channel exceeds roughly 50-60% of total budget
- [ ] Contractual or minimum-commitment constraints are identified and respected
- [ ] Scenario planning (best/base/worst case) is included when uncertainty is material (new channel, budget cut, aggressive target, macro volatility)
- [ ] For budget-cut scenarios, an efficiency-ranked or strategic-protection alternative is presented alongside any uniform cut

## Transparency

- [ ] All assumptions are listed explicitly in a dedicated section
- [ ] All data gaps are identified, along with the benchmark or estimate used in place of missing data
- [ ] Attribution method used for channel-level figures is stated
- [ ] Measurement/time window used for each metric is stated

## Sign-Off Readiness

- [ ] Model has been reviewed against `checklists/quality_checklist.md`
- [ ] Prepared-by, reviewed-by, and approved-by fields are completed
- [ ] Output is in a format usable directly by finance/marketing stakeholders without further translation
