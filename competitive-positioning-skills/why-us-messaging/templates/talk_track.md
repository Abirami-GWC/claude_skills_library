# Talk Track Template — [Our Company] vs. [Competitor] — [Persona Label]

> Written to be spoken aloud on a call. Short sentences. No jargon. One breath per line.

**If the prospect brings up [Competitor] first:**

"[Competitor]'s a name we see a lot — [one-sentence honest acknowledgment]."

"Where we tend to pull ahead is [specific gap], especially for [persona label]s
who care about [persona priority]."

"[One concrete proof point], if that's useful to hear more about."

**If you're the one raising the comparison:**

"A lot of [persona label]s evaluating [category] also look at [Competitor] —
worth knowing where we're different for someone in your role specifically."

"[Differentiator 1, framed as an outcome for this persona]."

**Bridge back to discovery (always end here):**

"What matters most for you — is it [dimension A relevant to persona] or
[dimension B]?"

---
### Notes for the rep
- Don't volunteer more than 2 differentiators unprompted.
- If asked something without a sourced answer, say "let me confirm and follow
  up" rather than guessing.
