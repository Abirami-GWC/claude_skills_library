# Templates

## Purpose
Reusable, fillable templates for briefing and building email sequences. Placeholders use `[brackets]`.

---

## 1. Email Sequence Brief
- **Sequence type**: [welcome / nurture / onboarding / win-back / cart abandon / post-purchase / launch]
- **Goal**: [e.g., convert trial users to paid]
- **Audience/persona**: [e.g., marketing managers at mid-size B2B SaaS companies]
- **Offer/product**: [name + one-line description]
- **Tone**: [e.g., warm and conversational / professional and concise]
- **Sequence length**: [number of emails, or "use standard"]
- **Cadence constraints**: [e.g., must fit within 14 days]
- **Existing assets to reuse**: [testimonials, case studies, brand guide link]
- **Primary CTA destination**: [URL/action]

---

## 2. Single Email Template
**Email [#] of [total] — Strategic role: [e.g., "build trust with social proof"]**
- Subject line: [option A]
- Subject line (alt): [option B]
- Preview text: [text]
- Send timing: [e.g., Day 3]
- Body:
  [Opening line — personalized/contextual hook]
  [1-2 short paragraphs delivering the email's core content]
  [Optional social proof / example]
  CTA: [button text]
- P.S. (optional): [reinforce CTA or add one small extra value]
