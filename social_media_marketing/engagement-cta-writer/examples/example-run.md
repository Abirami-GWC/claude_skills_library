# Example Run

**User input:** "Here's my post about cutting our pricing tiers from 7 to 3, conversions went up 22%. Need a CTA, want comments."

## Output produced

**Goal:** Comments

**Option 1 — Comment-driving (specific number)**
"What's your tier count right now?"

**Option 2 — Comment-driving (opinion/debate)**
"Disagree that fewer options = more conversions? Tell me why."

**Option 3 — Comment-driving (personal experience)**
"Has anyone else simplified pricing and seen the opposite happen?"

---
Recommendation: Option 1 — it's the lowest-effort, most specific ask (a number, not an opinion), which tends to pull the highest comment volume on results-driven posts like this one.
