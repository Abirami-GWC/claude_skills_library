# 4-Touch Email Sequence Skeleton

Use as a fast starting skeleton for SMB/mid-market deals. Replace every bracketed
placeholder — do not leave any `[...]` in the final delivered copy.

---

## Touch 1 — Day 0 (Specific hook)
**Subject:** [specific, lowercase-feeling, tied to a real signal]

Hi [First Name],

[One sentence referencing a specific, verified fact about their company/role —
a launch, a hire, a piece of content, a peer's use case.]

[One sentence connecting that fact to a problem your offer addresses.]

[Soft, curiosity-based CTA — a question, not a meeting ask.]

[Sender name]

---

## Touch 2 — Day 3 (New value angle)
**Subject:** [different angle, not "following up"]

Hi [First Name],

[A different reason this might matter to them — a proof point, stat, or
mini-insight not mentioned in touch 1.]

[Optional: one-line relevant example, marked [VERIFY: ...] if not confirmed.]

[Slightly clearer CTA.]

[Sender name]

---

## Touch 3 — Day 7 (Give without asking)
**Subject:** [value-forward, e.g. "a resource, not a pitch"]

Hi [First Name],

[Share something useful on its own merits — an article, a short insight, a
relevant benchmark — with no hard sell attached.]

[Very light, optional CTA: "Happy to share more if useful."]

[Sender name]

---

## Touch 4 — Day 12 (Breakup)
**Subject:** [clear signal this is the last touch, e.g. "closing the loop"]

Hi [First Name],

[Acknowledge you haven't connected yet, without guilt-tripping.]

[Leave the door open — one line making it easy for them to reach out later.]

[No CTA pressure — optional single low-friction reply option.]

[Sender name]
