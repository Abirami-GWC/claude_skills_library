# Email Body Templates by Angle

## Hook-Bridge-Value-CTA (default touch 1 structure)
```
Hi [First Name],

[Hook — specific fact about them/their company]. [Bridge — why that likely
matters/hurts right now].

[Value — one sentence on how the offer helps, framed as outcome not feature].

[CTA — soft question]?

[Sender]
```

## Give-touch (no ask)
```
Hi [First Name],

[Share a genuinely useful, standalone insight/resource relevant to their role —
no mention of the product yet].

[Optional one line: "Thought this might be useful given [reason]."]

[Sender]

P.S. Happy to send more like this if useful — no pressure either way.
```

## Proof-point touch
```
Hi [First Name],

[One sentence: a peer company/persona in a similar position — only if real and
confirmed, otherwise mark [VERIFY: peer example]].

[One sentence: the specific outcome they saw, in the persona's terms — e.g. time
saved, cost avoided, risk reduced].

[CTA — slightly clearer than touch 1, still low-pressure]?

[Sender]
```

## Question-only micro-touch (for touch 2 in fast/SMB cadences)
```
Hi [First Name] — [one sharp, specific question tied to their likely situation]?

[Sender]
```
Keep this one to 1-2 sentences total. Works well as a pattern-interrupt after a
longer touch 1.
