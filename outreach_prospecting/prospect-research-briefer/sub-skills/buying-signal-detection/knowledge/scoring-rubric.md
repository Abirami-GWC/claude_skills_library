# Signal Scoring Rubric

Score each candidate signal 1-3 on each axis, sum for a total out of 9.

| Score | Specificity | Recency | Offer relevance |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Generic to the whole industry | Older than the typical window for its type | No plausible connection to the offer |
| 2 | Somewhat specific to this company | Within window but not fresh | Indirect/plausible connection |
| 3 | Uniquely specific to this company/contact | Fresh, within the last few weeks | Directly maps to the offer's core value prop |

**Keep for the brief:** total score ≥ 6.
**Mention as a footnote only:** total score 4-5.
**Drop:** total score ≤ 3.

## Worked example
Signal: "Acme posted 6 backend engineering job listings this month, several
mentioning data pipeline work."
- Specificity: 3 (specific listings, specific company)
- Recency: 3 (this month)
- Offer relevance: 3 (offer is ETL/data pipeline tooling)
- Total: 9 → strong primary signal, use as the touch-1 hook.

Signal: "Acme is a fast-growing SaaS company."
- Specificity: 1 (true of thousands of companies)
- Recency: 1 (not time-bound at all)
- Offer relevance: 1 (no specific connection)
- Total: 3 → drop.
