# Connection Note Examples (annotated)

## Good
> "Hi Priya — saw the note on schema drift issues during the migration. Would love
> to connect and swap notes." (108 characters)
Specific, no pitch, earns the accept.

## Bad — pitches
> "Hi Priya, we help data teams cut ETL downtime by 50%. Would love to connect and
> tell you more!" (98 characters, but pitches)
Fails the no-pitch rule even though it's within the character limit.

## Bad — too generic
> "Hi Priya, I'd love to add you to my professional network." (60 characters)
Passes the character limit but is generic enough to send to literally anyone —
lower accept rate, doesn't demonstrate real interest.
