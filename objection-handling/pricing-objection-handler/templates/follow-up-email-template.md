# Follow-up email template — pricing objections

Post-call follow-up for price/budget pushback handled via
`handlers/pricing-objection-handler.md`. Fill the bracketed fields; personalize before sending.

## After a pricing objection

```
Subject: TCO breakdown from our conversation

Hi [Name],

Following up on the cost question from today. Attached is a breakdown that includes
[incident response time / staff hours / downtime cost — whichever was discussed], not just the
license price, since that was the comparison you were making.

I don't have discount authority to quote a number over email, but I've looped in [pricing/deal
desk contact] who can walk through options once we're aligned on scope.

[Rep name]
```

**Note:** consistent with the hard rule in `handlers/pricing-objection-handler.md`, this template
never states a discount figure — it routes to the approved pricing owner. The same restriction
applies to renewal-pricing follow-ups; see `playbooks/renewal-and-upsell-objections.md`.

*(Illustrative for a network-security sales context — adjust tone and specifics before production
use.)*
