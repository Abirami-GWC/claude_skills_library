# Follow-up email template — post-stall check-in

Not a roleplay artifact itself, but the real-world follow-up for the pattern practiced against
`playbooks/negotiation-endgame-playbook.md` — useful once a rep has rehearsed the conversation and
needs to actually send something after a deal goes quiet post-verbal-agreement.

## After an endgame stall

```
Subject: Checking in — anything blocking signature I can help with?

Hi [Name],

Wanted to check in directly rather than just following up generically — is there anything
specific blocking signature right now that I can help unblock, whether that's another
stakeholder, a redline, or something else?

Happy to jump on a quick call if that's easier than email.

[Rep name]
```

*(Illustrative for a network-security sales context — adjust tone and specifics before production
use.)*
