# DM Sequence Skeleton

## DM 1 — Immediately after accept
"Thanks for connecting, [Name]! [One line continuing the connection note's thread
— a follow-up question or comment, not a pitch]."

## DM 2 — 2-4 days later
"[One line sharing a relevant insight, resource, or observation]. [Optional light
question]."

## DM 3 — 4-6 days after DM 2
"[One line transitioning to the actual reason for reaching out, framed as a
question/offer]. [Low-friction ask]."

## DM 4 (optional) — Light breakup
"No worries if now's not the right time, [Name] — happy to reconnect whenever's useful."
