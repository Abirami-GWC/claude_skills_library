# Connection Note Templates

## Specific-signal note (cold)
`Hi [Name] — [specific fact: post/launch/hire/content]. Would love to connect.`
Example: "Hi Priya — saw your post on schema drift during migrations. Would love to connect."

## Mutual connection note
`Hi [Name] — [Mutual Contact] suggested we connect given [shared relevant context].`
Example: "Hi Priya — Sam Lee mentioned we're both deep in the data reliability space. Would love to connect."

## Shared event/group note
`Hi [Name] — great [session/talk] at [event]. Would love to stay connected.`
Example: "Hi Priya — great talk at DataEng Summit on pipeline observability. Would love to stay connected."

## Content-engagement note
`Hi [Name] — really resonated with your take on [specific point from their content]. Would love to connect.`
