# Breakup Email Examples

## Good example
> Hi Priya,
>
> Haven't heard back, so I'll assume now isn't the right time to dig into the
> pipeline reliability stuff — totally understand, priorities shift.
>
> If that changes down the line, happy to pick this back up whenever's useful.
> Feel free to just reply "not now" and I'll close this out on my end.
>
> — Dev

## Bad example (guilt-tripping — do not produce this)
> Hi Priya,
>
> I've reached out several times now with no response. I guess this just isn't a
> priority for your team right now. I'll stop reaching out.

**Why it's bad:** passive-aggressive tone, implies blame, no genuine door left open.

## Bad example (fake urgency — do not produce this)
> Hi Priya,
>
> This is my final email — after this, the offer we discussed will no longer be
> available.

**Why it's bad:** manufactures false scarcity when no real time-limited offer
exists; damages trust and brand if the prospect later notices the "offer" was
never actually withdrawn.
