# Company Research Checklist

- [ ] What does the company do (1-2 sentence description)?
- [ ] Industry / vertical
- [ ] Approximate size (headcount) and funding stage, with date noted
- [ ] Any funding round in the last ~12 months? Amount, date, source.
- [ ] Any leadership changes in the last ~6 months?
- [ ] Any product launches or major announcements in the last ~3 months?
- [ ] Any M&A activity (acquired, acquiring, merging)?
- [ ] Hiring trend in functions relevant to the offer (e.g., data roles for a data
      product) — check job postings.
- [ ] Any publicly inferable tech stack signals relevant to the offer?
- [ ] Headcount growth trend (roughly flat / growing / shrinking) if determinable.

Mark each checked item with its source and date. If an item can't be found after a
reasonable search, note it in the brief's "Gaps" section rather than leaving it
blank with no explanation.
