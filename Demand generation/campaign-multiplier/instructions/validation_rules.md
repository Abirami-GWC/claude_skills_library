# Validation Rules — Campaign Multiplier

Run before delivering any campaign brief.

## Strategic coherence checks

- [ ] 1–3 messaging pillars defined, and every channel's angle ties back to at
      least one of them.
- [ ] No two channels share an identical angle + CTA without a noted reason
      (e.g., intentional repetition for frequency).
- [ ] Every included channel maps to the stated goal via
      `knowledge/channel_playbook.md`'s selection heuristic — no channel
      included "just because."
- [ ] Excluded channels (if any relevant ones weren't chosen) are listed with
      a one-line reason, not silently dropped.

## Grounding checks

- [ ] No assumed budget, team capacity, or channel access beyond what the
      user confirmed.
- [ ] No fabricated audience research or performance benchmarks — targets
      not sourced from the user are marked "target TBD."
- [ ] Timeframe-sensitive channels (paid ads, webinars) have their lead-time
      needs flagged if the stated timeframe is tight.

## Scope checks

- [ ] The brief stays at the angle/hook level — no fully drafted social posts,
      emails, or scripts (that's `content-repurposer`'s job).
- [ ] Each channel entry has a success metric — no channel included without a
      way to measure it.

## Final gate

If any grounding-check box is unresolved, mark the relevant field "TBD —
needs input" rather than guessing, and list it under Open Questions in the brief.
