# Glossary

## Purpose
Shared terminology for landing page and conversion copywriting.

- **Hero section**: The top-of-page area containing headline, subheadline, primary visual, and first CTA.
- **Above the fold**: Content visible without scrolling.
- **5-second test**: Whether a visitor can understand the offer and audience within 5 seconds of landing.
- **Social proof**: Evidence from others (testimonials, logos, stats, reviews) supporting a claim.
- **Objection handling**: Copy that proactively addresses reasons a visitor might hesitate to convert.
- **Risk reversal**: Guarantees, free trials, or easy-cancellation language that reduces perceived risk.
- **Conversion goal**: The single primary action the page is designed to drive.
- **Micro-copy**: Small supporting text near a CTA or form that reduces friction/hesitation.
- **Squeeze page**: A minimal landing page built around a single opt-in action.
- **Long-form sales page**: An extended page structure used for high-consideration or high-price offers.

## Summary
Consistent terminology ensures landing page copy outputs are easy to hand off to designers, developers, and marketing teams.
