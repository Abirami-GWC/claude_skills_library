# Follow-up email template — competitor objections

Post-call follow-up for calls where a competitor was name-dropped, handled via
`handlers/competitor-objection-handler.md`. Fill the bracketed fields; personalize before
sending.

## After a competitor objection

```
Subject: Following up re: [Competitor name] comparison

Hi [Name],

Wanted to follow up on the comparison to [Competitor] from today's call. As discussed,
[differentiation angle from handlers/competitor-objection-handler.md] tends to matter most for
teams evaluating both — happy to set up a working session with your team to go through it side by
side if useful.

[Rep name]
```

**Note:** only include competitor claims verified in the battlecard table in
`handlers/competitor-objection-handler.md` — don't add new competitor facts in the email that
weren't confirmed on the call.

*(Illustrative for a network-security sales context — adjust tone and specifics before production
use.)*
