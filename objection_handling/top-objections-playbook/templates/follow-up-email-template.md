# Follow-up email templates — playbook objections

Post-call follow-up emails for general (non-pricing, non-competitor) objections handled via
`handlers/top-objections-playbook.md`. Fill the bracketed fields; personalize at least the
opening line to something specific from the actual call rather than sending these unedited.

## After a general playbook objection (e.g. "need to think about it")

```
Subject: Following up on [specific concern discussed]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the time today. You mentioned [specific concern from the call] — I wanted to follow up
with [relevant resource: case study / one-pager / answer] on that specifically, rather than just
checking in generically.

[One sentence connecting the resource back to what they said.]

Let me know if it's helpful, or if there's a different concern I should be addressing instead.

[Rep name]
```

## After a procurement/legal blocker (playbook row #10)

```
Subject: Pre-packaged info for your procurement review

Hi [Name],

Ahead of your procurement/legal review, I've attached what's typically requested
([security questionnaire / SOC 2 report / MSA redline history — adjust to what's actually
available]) so your team has a head start.

Let me know what else procurement usually needs and I'll get it over.

[Rep name]
```

*(Illustrative for a network-security sales context — adjust tone and specifics to the team's
actual voice before production use.)*
