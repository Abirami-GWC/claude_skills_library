# Worked Examples

## 1. Full proposal — Alderbrook Health Network

**Rate card (filled in for this example)**: Insight Suite Core = $60,000/yr; Advanced Analytics add-on = $25,000/yr; 2-year term discount = 10%; Net 30; 30-day validity; 6-week onboarding.

**Input**: "Build a proposal for Alderbrook Health Network — Insight Suite Core plus the Advanced Analytics add-on, 2-year term, use our standard rate card and terms."

**Output (proposal content)**:

Cover: proposal for Alderbrook Health Network, prepared for Priya Anand (VP of Data) — replacing manual, spreadsheet-based reporting across care sites with automated dashboards.

Scope: Insight Suite Core (unified data ingestion/dashboarding), Advanced Analytics add-on (predictive reporting, anomaly detection).

Pricing:

| Item | Unit price | Term | Discount | Total (2-yr) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Insight Suite Core | $60,000/yr | 2 yrs | 10% | $108,000 |
| Advanced Analytics add-on | $25,000/yr | 2 yrs | 10% | $45,000 |
| **Total** | $85,000/yr list | | | **$153,000** |

Terms: Net 30, valid through [send date + 30 days], onboarding begins within 2 weeks, 6-week implementation. Next step: signed order form.

## 2. Quote-only — quick request, no full proposal needed

**Input**: "Just need a quick quote for Northline Manufacturing — 100 seats of Insight Suite Core, annual, no add-ons."

**Output** (using `quote_only_template.md`, no case study, no multi-section structure):

| Item | Unit price | Term | Discount | Total |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Insight Suite Core (100 seats) | $60,000/yr | 1 yr | — | $60,000 |
| **Total** | | | | **$60,000** |

Payment terms: Net 30. Valid through [date].

## Why these are correct

The full proposal pulls every number from the filled-in rate card and shows the discount math explicitly rather than a single final figure. The quote-only example correctly skips the multi-section structure (no cover paragraph, no case study, no signature block) because the user asked for a quick number, not a formal proposal — matching the lighter `quote_only_template.md` to the lighter request.
