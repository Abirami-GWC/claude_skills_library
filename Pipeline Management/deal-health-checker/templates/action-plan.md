# Action Plan Template

A concrete action table for every Warning and At Risk deal — this is what turns a
diagnosis into something the rep actually does today.

## Layout

```
| Deal | Immediate Action | Reason | Priority | Expected Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ABC Manufacturing | Call the customer directly | 18 days of silence — email alone hasn't worked | High | Re-establish contact and confirm the deal is still live |
| ABC Manufacturing | Identify the decision maker | Currently unknown — can't confirm this deal can actually close | High | A named economic buyer to engage directly |
| Gamma LLC | Confirm decision maker and get a response on next steps | Both currently unstated — can't assess real risk without this | Medium | Clear read on whether this deal is progressing |
```

## Rules

- One row per action, not one row per deal — a single At Risk deal often needs 2-3
  distinct actions.
- Every action must trace back to a specific risk factor or missing-information
  item from that deal's report — no generic "check in more" actions.
- Don't generate action rows for Healthy deals — a clean bill of health doesn't
  need an action plan.
