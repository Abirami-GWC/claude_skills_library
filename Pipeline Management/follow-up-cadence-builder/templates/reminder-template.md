# Reminder Template

A short line per scheduled touch, suitable for pasting directly into a CRM task or
task manager. Not a message draft — just the actionable reminder.

## Pattern

`Follow up with [Deal Name] via [Channel] on [Date] — [Purpose, 3-6 words]`

## Examples

- `Follow up with Northwind Traders via Email on Jul 16 — check in on proposal`
- `Follow up with Northwind Traders via Call on Jul 19 — ask about timeline directly`
- `Follow up with Northwind Traders via Email on Jul 22 — break-up / close out check`

## Overdue marker

If a scheduled date has already passed by the time this plan is generated, prefix
the line with `OVERDUE —` so it's visually distinct:

- `OVERDUE — Follow up with Beta Inc via Email on Jul 10 — check in on proposal`

Don't silently reschedule an overdue touch to today without saying so — flag it as
overdue and let the rep decide whether to send it immediately or adjust.
