# Example Run

**User input:** "We just launched a new onboarding flow that cut signup drop-off by 40%. Need LinkedIn and X ideas. Our voice is direct, a little blunt, no corporate fluff."

**Angle selection (from mapping):** Product update → data-led, behind-the-scenes, direct value.

## Output produced

### LinkedIn

**Idea 1 — Data-led**
Hook: "We cut signup drop-off by 40% by deleting a single form field."
Angle: Walk through the one field that was killing conversions, why it was there in the first place (legal wanted it), and how we resolved it without removing the underlying need.
Format: text-only

**Idea 2 — Behind-the-scenes**
Hook: "Our old onboarding flow had 11 steps. Nobody could tell us why."
Angle: Story of auditing the flow, finding steps nobody remembered adding, and the internal pushback to cutting them.
Format: text-only

**Idea 3 — Direct value**
Hook: "Here's the exact 3-question audit we ran on our onboarding before rebuilding it."
Angle: Turn the audit process into a mini-framework readers can apply to their own product.
Format: carousel

### X / Twitter

**Idea 1**
Tweet: "We deleted one form field. Signup drop-off dropped 40%. The field was asking for a phone number nobody used."
Format: single tweet

**Idea 2 — Thread**
Opener: "Our onboarding flow had 11 steps. We got it to 4. Drop-off fell 40%. Here's what we cut and why each step existed in the first place:"
Format: thread

---
Note how each hook is specific to the real number and real detail given — not a generic template with blanks filled in.
