# Compliance Playbook

## Pain: Difficulty proving compliance / audit prep is painful

**Demo Flow**
1. Show automated compliance reporting
2. Show audit log retention & search
3. Show policy management / enforcement dashboard

**Business Value**
Reduced audit prep time; fewer findings; continuous compliance posture.

## Pain: No consistent policy enforcement across the org

**Demo Flow**
1. Show centralized policy management
2. Show policy violation alerting
3. Show remediation workflow

**Business Value**
Consistent security posture; reduced risk of policy drift.

## Sequencing note
Compliance demos work best paired with whatever underlying technical pain
(security or networking) is driving the compliance concern — show the technical
fix first, then the compliance reporting as evidence the fix is auditable.
