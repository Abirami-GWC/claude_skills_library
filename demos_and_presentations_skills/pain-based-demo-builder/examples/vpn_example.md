# Example: VPN + Visibility

**Input**
Customer: Bank
Pain Points:
- Slow/unreliable VPN
- Poor visibility across branches

**Output**
Demo Agenda
1. Reliable VPN connectivity
2. Centralized monitoring dashboard
3. Reporting for IT leadership
4. Q&A

**Why this order:** Connectivity is foundational and was the more urgent pain (banks
are highly sensitive to remote-access downtime). Visibility is the quick, visible
win shown next. Reporting closes the loop for leadership before Q&A.
