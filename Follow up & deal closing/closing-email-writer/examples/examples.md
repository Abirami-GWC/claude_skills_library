# Worked Examples

Running scenario: GWC Data selling Insight Suite to Alderbrook Health Network (contact: Priya Anand, VP of Data). Proposal sent June 20, $85K/year, 2-year term.

## 1. Proposal nudge

**Input**: "Write a nudge email to Priya about the Alderbrook proposal — it's been quiet since we sent it June 20, and she mentioned the CFO still needs to sign off on budget."

**Output**:

> Subject: Following up on Alderbrook's Insight Suite proposal
>
> Hi Priya,
>
> Wanted to check in on the proposal I sent over on June 20 for Insight Suite. I know budget sign-off with your CFO was the next step — happy to put together a short ROI summary you can forward, or jump on a quick call with him directly if that would help move things along.
>
> Let me know what would be most useful — otherwise I'll plan to check back in next week.
>
> Best, [Your name]

## 2. Contract chase

**Input**: "Alderbrook signed off verbally but the order form we sent August 3rd still isn't back. No real deadline, just want a gentle nudge."

**Output**:

> Subject: Quick nudge — Insight Suite order form still needs a signature
>
> Hi Priya,
>
> Just following up on the order form sent on August 3rd — looks like it's still waiting on a signature. Happy to hop on a call if there's anything to clarify before signing, or if legal has any questions on their end.
>
> Let me know if there's anything blocking it — happy to help move it along.
>
> [Your name]

(Note: no deadline line included, since none was given — never invent one.)

## 3. Re-engagement

**Input**: "Northline Manufacturing went quiet after a great demo three weeks ago. Write something to re-engage them."

**Output**:

> Subject: Still the right time?
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> Haven't heard back since our demo three weeks ago — totally understand priorities shift. Wanted to check whether Insight Suite is still worth pursuing right now, or if timing has changed on your end.
>
> If it's still relevant, happy to pick back up with a quick call. If not, no worries at all — just let me know either way.
>
> [Your name]

## 4. Recap

**Output**:

> Subject: Recap from our call today
>
> Hi [Name],
