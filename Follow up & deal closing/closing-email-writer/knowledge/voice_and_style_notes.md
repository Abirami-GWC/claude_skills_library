# Voice & Style Notes

This skill produces the *structure and content* of the email (per `email_guidelines.md` and the templates). A saved `my-writing-style` profile changes *how it sounds* — word choice, sentence rhythm, level of formality, typical openers/closers.

## What style is allowed to change

- Sentence length and rhythm.
- Formality level (e.g. "Hey" vs. "Hi").
- Preferred transition phrases.
- Sign-off style.

## What style is never allowed to change

- The factual claims in the email (what was sent, when, what's being asked).
- Whether a deadline/urgency line is included — that's governed by whether a real deadline exists, not by voice.
- The required structural elements (subject, one clear ask, close).

## Workflow

1. If `my-writing-style` is listed among available skills, load it before drafting and write directly in that voice.
2. If only `setup-writing-style` is listed, draft in the default guidelines voice, then add one line offering to learn the user's style.
3. If the user edits the draft or says it doesn't sound like them, offer to save the specific change to `my-writing-style` — do not suggest rerunning the full setup.
